Emergency Support

If you or someone you know is in immediate danger, call emergency services (e.g., 14416 all over India) right away. This page is for informational purposes and not a substitute for professional crisis intervention.

Crisis Hotlines

  • Tamil Nadu:Call 14416 - 24/7 support.
  • Kerala:Call 14416 (24/7).
  • Karanataka:Call 14416.
  • Andhra Pradesh:Call 14416
  • International: Visit Befrienders Worldwide for local hotlines.

Quick Tips for Immediate Help

  • ✓ Breathe deeply: Inhale for 4 seconds, hold for 4, exhale for 4.
  • ✓ Reach out: Talk to a trusted friend or family member.
  • ✓ Ground yourself: Name 5 things you can see, 4 you can touch, etc.
  • ✓ Seek professional help: Contact a counsellor through our chat or local services.
